What makes a community?

New Media Club is a student run organization at RIT, and often serves as the beating heart of our New Media community. The club works to connect students throughout their college experience, alumni, and professors outside of a classroom setting. The mentorship program is also an integral part of the club experience, helping to bolster relationships, make unlikely friends, and instill confidence throughout the community. The organization has been passed down from generation to generation within the school, and as design evolves, so does the club.

The Challenge

The world of design is constantly changing, and our diverse community reflects these constant evolutions. As the 2020 Elected Board members, we worked to bring the spirit of new media to the forefront of our new branding, highlighting creativity, interaction, and the history of design that brought us to where we are now.

A Playful Perspective

As we worked to build our new vision for New Media Club, interactions came to define many of our design decisions. Creating a brand that was able to capture the playful, spirited, and colorful nature of our community was our primary goal. We created the new homepage with this in mind, filling each section with something that allowed visitors to explore these facets of New Media Club.

A New Face

We worked tirelessly to explore every possible direction for the new New Media Club logo, and ended right where we started. The new symbol for the club embodies everything the club stands for, and represents changing screens, the motion of interaction, and movement towards the future.
